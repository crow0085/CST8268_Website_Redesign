Our Mission

In 1985 the Nepean Chamber of Commerce established the Nepean Sports Wall of Fame. Our goal was then and remains now ...

  • To recognize attained excellence and athletic achievement by Nepean athletes and athletic teams at the national and international level of competition.
  • To stimulate awareness and pride of the role the City of Nepean's facilities and athletes have had on the national and international sporting scene.
  • To create a tangible and permanent historic collage of significant sporting accomplishments for mass viewing.
  • To induce and encourage the youth of Nepean to pursue athletic excellence through recognition of these athletic achievers.
